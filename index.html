Recommend R Books for Brother Sen

Welcome to my personal blog. I have been reading some R books recently, and I want to share my experiments with you.

R for Everyone is the prescribed textbook in your class, which have already accumulated many 4-stars and 5-stars reviews on Amazon. It have not been introduced to China, so I just readed the contents and reviews from audiences. RfE is new, and it deals with some packages which were developed recently. You have told me that this book involved the usage of various packages only, lacking in programming. This book, however, is for beginners, not for intermediate or advanced readers who will take advantage of the programming features. RfE is readable and accessible for you, and maybe you have to read it carefully and thoroughly in order to get an A grade.
